Our services

Our repairs cost $15 an hour for labor plus the cost of any additional parts we may need and instal or replace to ensure your bicycle is properly working again.
Paint jobs
We also offer top of the line professional paint jobs. We charge $50 for a new paint job in the colour of your choice plus $5 for each decal you may want to add.
